About Cedar Belles

Cedar Belles Wedding Grove is named in honor of our children, Cedar and Belle. Our little grove is a place built on love, family, and connection — a quiet outdoor space for couples who want a wedding that feels personal rather than overwhelming.

With touches of Celtic tradition, Pacific Northwest greenery, and a calm, relaxed approach, Cedar Belles specializes in micro-weddings, elopements, and intimate ceremonies up to 10 guests.

FAQ

How many guests can we have?

At this time, Cedar Belles specializes in micro-weddings with up to 10 guests plus the couple.

Do you provide alcohol?

No. Couples may bring their own champagne, beer, or wine for self-service. We can provide glassware and setup, but we do not serve or supply alcohol.

What if it rains?

Ceremonies proceed in light rain if the couple is comfortable. In cases of unsafe weather, we allow one complimentary reschedule to a mutually available date.

When is payment due?

A 30% non-refundable retainer is due at booking. The remaining balance is due 7 days before your ceremony date.
